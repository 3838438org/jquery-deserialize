h1. jQuery.deserialize

Deserializes strings, arrays or objects and populates a form with that data.

h2. What?

Think of it as the opposite of the "jQuery.serialize()":http://api.jquery.com/serialize, "jQuery.serializeArray()":http://api.jquery.com/serializeArray or "jQuery.serializeObject()":http://benalman.com/projects/jquery-misc-plugins/#serializeobject functions.
